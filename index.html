About the event

Stackathon is a form of rapid prototyping organized to develop solutions in a safe environment and expedited time frame by newly established developers. Stackathon is a 48-hour hackathon. Our hackathon focuses on bringing together members of the greater community.
Stackathon is for all kinds of developers and students looking to take their skills further and it will be conducted through online from MAY 15 5PM TO MAY 17 6PM. Join as a team of minimum 3 and maximum 5 members.

The use of Stackathon is that

  • Can create new concepts and ideas
  • Talent acquisition
  • Product development
  • Add value to resume or CV
  • Can gain experience of a hackathon

The winners will be awarded as
  • 1ST PRIZE - 3RD MONTH COURSE FOR FREE
  • 2ND PRIZE - 60% OFF FOR 4TH MONTH
  • 3RD PRIZE- 40% OFF FOR 5TH MONTH
